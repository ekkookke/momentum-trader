# Keep scope long-only A-share ETF momentum

Accepted. Momentum Trader is for a retail investor who wants disciplined A-share ETF momentum trading and market signal scanning. We will keep the product long-only and ETF-focused, explicitly excluding leverage, shorting, futures, and high-frequency trading so the system remains understandable, repeatable, and operationally light.

**Considered Options**

- Include broader quant instruments such as futures, shorting, leverage, or high-frequency execution.
- Keep the scope to long-only ETF trend following and signal scanning.

**Consequences**

The project should push back on features that optimize for institutional quant competition at the cost of execution discipline, traceability, or retail-operational simplicity.
